An "Innovation Tinker Kit" is an electronics kit designed for educational purposes and prototyping. These kits are often aimed at beginners or those new to electronics and programming and typically include a range of components and modules that can be used to build various electronic projects. The idea is to encourage tinkering, experimentation, and learning about electronics and programming in a hands-on way.
